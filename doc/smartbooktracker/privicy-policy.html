Privacy Policy – Smart Bookmark Tracker

Last updated: May 16, 2025

Overview

Smart Bookmark Tracker is a Chrome Extension that helps users track time spent on websites and recommend bookmarks based on browsing behavior. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how we protect it.

Data We Collect

  • URLs and domains of visited websites
  • Timestamps for when tabs are opened, closed, or switched
  • Time spent on each website
  • Google account email and profile (only when you sign in)

Why We Collect This Data

  • To analyze your focus level during browsing
  • To suggest frequently visited websites as bookmarks
  • To sync your usage data to your account (if signed in)
  • To improve user experience and provide statistics

How Your Data Is Protected

  • Your data is only visible to you.
  • Local data is stored securely using Chrome’s storage API.
  • Signed-in users have their data synced via HTTPS to a secure backend.
  • We do not sell, share, or expose your data to third parties.

Permissions We Use & Why

Permission Purpose
tabs To detect tab switches, openings, and closings
storage To store session data locally
history To retrieve visit counts and recommend bookmarks
identity To allow sign-in via Google
webNavigation To detect in-page URL changes (e.g., for SPA sites)
alarms To periodically save your data in the background
<all_urls> To track which websites you visit for time calculation (no content is read)

Google Sign-In

If you sign in using Google, we only access:

  • Your email address
  • Your name
  • Your profile picture

This information is used to associate browsing data with your account and provide personalized results. We do not access your contacts, calendar, or any sensitive account data.

We Do NOT:

  • Inject scripts into pages
  • Read or modify webpage content
  • Track keystrokes or input
  • Use third-party trackers or ads
